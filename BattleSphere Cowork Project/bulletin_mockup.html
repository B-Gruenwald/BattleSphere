Act II — The Fractured Subsector · Dispatch No. 7
The Tide Turns at Stiria

The war for the Austriacus Subsector enters its third and bloodiest week. Across the void, fleet engagements have reduced the once-proud orbital platforms of Stiria to drifting wreckage, while on the surface, a dozen factions contest every hab-block and promethium line.

The fall of the relay station at Salis has severed vox-contact between the eastern and western theatres. Each commander now fights blind, trusting only the soldiers at their side and whatever intelligence their scouts can wrest from the enemy dead.

In the ruins of Liberta Judex, the Genestealer Cult of the Ascending Dawn has surfaced openly for the first time. Their numbers, long cultivated in the underhive, proved enough to overwhelm two Imperial outposts in a single night — and the horror of their revelation has sent tremors of dread through every loyalist position.

Further west, Traitor Astartes warband elements have pushed hard against the mountain passes above Anterior Mons. Loyalist defenders held the first assault, but at severe cost. The passes remain contested.

Stranger still are the reports from the deep desert. Patrol units speak of Xenos forces long dormant now stirring beneath the sands — ancient war-machines of unknown provenance reactivating without warning, their purpose inscrutable.

Meanwhile, the governor's Strategi convenes in emergency session. Whispers of treachery at the highest levels of command have reached even the soldiers in the field. No-one knows whom to trust.

The Tide Turns at Stiria

The war for the Austriacus Subsector enters its third and bloodiest week. Across the void, fleet engagements have reduced the once-proud orbital platforms of Stiria to drifting wreckage, while on the surface, a dozen factions contest every hab-block and promethium line.
